How Transfers Work

  1. Select the product you want to move.
  2. Choose the source location and destination.
  3. Enter the quantity to transfer.
  4. Review the live preview showing stock after transfer.
  5. Click Initiate Transfer to confirm.
  6. The transfer appears in the log and inventory updates immediately.
